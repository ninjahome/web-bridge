Product, phase 1, Self Efficiency

Input two videos, SE Lab will automatically intercept the cycle of the action, identify the differences and mark them, and finally output a video with comparison

Product, phase 2, Optimized Solution

Compare individual user actions with professional athlete actions to continuously optimize the efficiency of amateur sports. Through continuous learning, SE Lab will develop the most suitable motion template for users.

For example, when I first use SE Lab, it will not provide me with Shohei Ohtani's template, but rather an optimized template for men who are 5'10" and 180 lbs.
